# Privacy Policy for 2048 - Multilingual

**Last Updated: February 07, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how karmalok.com ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application, 2048 - Multilingual (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## Information Collection and Use

The App is an offline puzzle game. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. All game data (scores, board state, and language preferences) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect limited technical data:

- **Expo Updates (expo.dev):** The App checks for over-the-air updates through Expo's update servers. During this process, technical information such as your device's IP address, platform, and app version may be logged by Expo. This data is governed by [Expo's Privacy Policy](https://expo.dev/privacy).

- **Expo Localization:** The App reads your device's language setting to provide a localized experience. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party services.

## Log Data

We do not collect any log data when you use the App.

## Cookies

The App does not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.

## Advertising and Analytics

The App does not contain any advertisements, analytics trackers, or usage monitoring tools.

## Local Storage

The App uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to save game progress and preferences. This data remains on your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information at risk. However, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of any information transmitted over the internet or stored on your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since the App does not collect any personal data from any user, it is suitable for use by individuals of all ages.
